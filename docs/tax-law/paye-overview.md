# PAYE Overview

Pay As You Earn (PAYE) applies to employment income and is generally withheld by employers.

## Employer Obligation

Employers are expected to compute, withhold, and remit PAYE within required deadlines.

## Employee Income

Taxable employment income can include salary, wages, allowances, benefits, and other gains from employment.

## Record Keeping

Employers should maintain payroll records and deduction schedules for audit support and reconciliation.
