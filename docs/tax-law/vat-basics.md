# Value Added Tax Basics

Uganda's VAT system generally taxes the supply of taxable goods and services and the importation of taxable goods.

## Standard Rate

The standard VAT rate is commonly applied at **18%**. Taxpayers should confirm current statutory rates for specific periods.

## Output Tax

Output tax is VAT charged by a registered person on taxable supplies.

## Input Tax

Input tax is VAT paid on purchases used for making taxable supplies and may be claimable subject to documentary and legal conditions.

## Invoices and Evidence

A valid tax invoice is a core document for claiming input tax credit. Missing or invalid invoices can result in disallowed credits.
