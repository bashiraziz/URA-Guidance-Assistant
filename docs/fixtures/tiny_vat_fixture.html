VAT Basics Fixture

VAT is chargeable on taxable supplies where conditions are met under the applicable law.

Section 1 Registration

Taxpayers may register for VAT where thresholds are met and must keep records.

Section 2 Invoices

Valid invoices support input tax claims and compliance checks.

Section 3 Filing

Returns are filed within prescribed timelines and payment is due with the return where applicable.

Section 4 Penalties

Late filing and under-declaration may attract interest and penalties.

Section 5 Appeals

Disputed assessments may be objected to in accordance with procedure rules.
